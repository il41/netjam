kinetics

in the virtual world of kinetics, users can get together and jam together on simulated and fictional instruments


make music together in virtual space :)
currently we have drums and a cube array



in the near future, multiplayer networks and avatars will be enabled, along with more instruments and sounds

planned improvements

currently, the sky is sound reactive, but reacts overly fast and will be smoothed in the future. its lighting will also be fixed

more instruments will be created with physical modeling and scripts. it would also be fun to add physics based instruments.
some sort of visual feedback on the object to show the sound has been played will also be implemented

the multiplayer aspect is the next major step for this project, enabling real-time music collaboration in a virtual world
